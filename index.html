    Our Donate Plan

    We will first give grants to the most affected people.
    After that we will start their rehabilitation process.

    Food for a family

    Food is one of the basic human needs. That demand increases even more during floods. Everyone has a role to play in meeting this human need.

    Shelter for a family

    Housing is one of the basic human needs. That demand became more widespread during the floods. As a human being, it is everyone responsibility to give shelter to homeless people.

    Education for a child

    Education is one of the basic rights of every child. There is no alternative to education if you want to develop as a real person. It is the moral responsibility of parents to educate every child in self-education.
